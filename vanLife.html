Vancouver life

For me Vancouver is one of the most beautiful cities I have been, The things I love most of this city is the amazing public transportation system, that for me is extremely efficient and most of the time buses are on time, and is really easy to pay a single trip and you can go everywhere in the city with the card of public transportation, this card is really cheap compare with the benefits that this card can give to you.

Other thing that I love in the city is the diversity of the people that lives here, changes the way you think, this diversity bring to the city new kind of ideas, like food, you can see everywhere here in vancouver restaurants from all of the world, in my home country was difficult to find different types of food that you can find here like indian food or korean food, I love that here we have tons of options.

Another factor that I treasure frome here is their nature, you have amazing views everywhere in this city and the weather is pretty good to do a lot of different activities here like sky, go to the beach, hiking, etc.. Also you can visit other cities that are pretty close to Vancouver, such as Victoria, Tofino, Whistler, the things here is there are a lot of places related to nature close to Vancouver.

For me one great activity that you can do here is go to a brewery, there are a lot around all the city and this places are amazing, breweries are growing in all bc in recent years, over 140 in 2017. Craft beer breweries has a big impact in job creation and in tourism (Bachman et al., 2021).
