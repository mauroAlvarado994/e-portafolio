About Me

I’m a proud Mexican from the northern part of Mexico, from a small town called Torreon that's close to one of the major cities of Mexico called Monterrey.

I have a bachelor's degree in Business Administration and a master's degree in Administration from the "Universidad Iberoamericana de Torreón". The thing I love the most about my degree is that it covers interesting topics like how to run a business, economics, and accounting.

In my free time, I love to barbecue and have a couple of beers, and when the weather is right, I enjoy riding my bike around Vancouver.

I’m a very curious man who enjoys traveling and meeting people from all around the world. Since I was a child, I have loved the way business is conducted in Canada and the US. I appreciate the way Canadian people think, respecting free time, each other, and enjoying diversity, something that you can only see in the developed world.
